Cade Blanco

Gameplay: 
Control the goblin & his cage to keep him from falling off of
the track. Try to collect as much cheese as possible and reach the
finish line before the time runs out!

Controls: 
W - Roll Forward 
S - Roll Backward 
A - Rotate Left 
D - Rotate Right 
ENTER - Pause / Resume
